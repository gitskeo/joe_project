About Me

I am a psychotherapist who sees myself as a partner in your development and in reaching your greatest fulfillment.

Having been a successful software sales executive, I know that there is so much more to life's purpose than just wealth and professional accolades.   I became a counselor to help clients navigate life and to get "unstuck" when the road ahead seems unclear or even hopeless. I'm a CBT and EMDR trained counselor, and have a wide depth of experience with different clients.   In session, you can expect to explore the roots of what’s really going on with you, and practice real tools we can use to improve life’s trajectory.
